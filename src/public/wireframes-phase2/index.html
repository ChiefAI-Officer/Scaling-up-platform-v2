Scaling Up Assessment Tool — Phase 2 Wireframes

Revisions + new screens per Jeff's May 8 email + May 12 scope-lock + Codex v7.4 rescope (hardcoded Website Assessment, sliders only, content-flagged Esperto-derived copy).

Plan version: v7.4 (May 12, 2026) Wave 1 scope: 9 screens Stakeholder: Jeff Verdun
What's new in Phase 2. Phase 1 covered the Coach-side wizard + campaign detail (10 desktop-only wireframes). Phase 2 revises three of those (per Jeff's May 8 per-screen feedback) and adds six new INVITED + PUBLIC participant flows. Several Phase 2 wireframes render mobile + desktop side-by-side because participant + leader-facing surfaces will be viewed on phones. Admin-side wireframes (User Setup) were dropped at v7.4 — those go straight to code.
