What happens next

After you click Create & Activate, the platform will:

  1. Create the campaign in ACTIVE status (or SCHEDULED if openAt is in the future).
  2. Generate a magic-link invitation row for each of the 18 participants (status: PENDING).
  3. Send 18 invitation emails immediately. Each contains a unique fragment-based token, valid until May 20, 2026.
  4. Flip each invitation to SENT on a successful email send. Failed sends stay PENDING and surface in the campaign detail with a "Retry" badge.
  5. Open the campaign detail page so you can monitor responses live.
