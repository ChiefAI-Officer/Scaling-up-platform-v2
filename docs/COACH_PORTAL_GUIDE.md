# Scaling Up Platform v2 - Coach Portal Guide

> Quick reference for coaches on using the self-service portal

---

## Getting Started

### Login
1. Navigate to `https://platform.scalingup.com/portal`
2. Sign in with your email (used for Circle.so)
3. First-time users: complete your profile setup

---

## Creating a Workshop

### Step 1: Request Workshop
1. Click **"Request New Workshop"** from Dashboard
2. Fill in:
   - Workshop Type (AI, Finish Strong, Basics)
   - Preferred Date (minimum 21 days out)
   - Format (Virtual or In-Person)
   - Location details (if in-person)

### Step 2: Certification Check
- ✅ **Auto-Approved**: If your certification is current
- ⏳ **Pending Review**: If certification expires within 30 days
- ❌ **Denied**: If certification is expired

### Step 3: Landing Page
Once approved, your landing page is auto-generated:
- Preview at **My Workshops → [Workshop] → View Landing Page**
- Share the link with attendees
- Registration link included

## Cancellation Policy

**14+ Days Before**: Full refund to attendees  
**< 14 Days Before**: Subject to $500 cancellation fee  

To cancel a workshop:
1. Navigate to **My Workshops → [Workshop]**
2. Click **"Request Cancellation"**
3. Provide reason
4. Wait for admin approval
